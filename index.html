Owner Story

About Mark:

My name is Mark, and I’m the owner of M&M Detailing, proudly serving Ruskin, Florida.

I started this business in 2022, but my passion for detailing goes back much further. Around age 14, I spent time working in my uncle’s classic car restoration shop, helping bring vehicles back to life and handling the detailing after restorations were complete.

Seeing those cars transformed, sometimes to even better than their original condition, is what made me fall in love with this work. Today, that same passion drives everything I do at M&M Detailing.

As someone with a military background, I’ve built this business on discipline, precision, and attention to detail. I take pride in delivering high-end results on every vehicle I touch, whether it’s a daily driver, a work truck, or a luxury or classic car.

At M&M Detailing, I offer complete auto detailing services designed to restore, protect, and enhance your vehicle inside and out. I don’t believe in cutting corners. I focus on the small details that make a big difference and leave your vehicle with that true showroom finish.

My goal is simple: deliver results that you notice immediately and service you can trust every time.

Because of my military background, I also have a deep respect for the people who serve our communities. That’s why I proudly offer discounts to military members, veterans, first responders, medical personnel, and educators. If you’re in Ruskin, FL or the surrounding areas and want your vehicle treated with precision, care, and pride, I’d be honored to take care of it.
